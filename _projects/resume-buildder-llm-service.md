Python-based LLM service using FastAPI and LangChain.  
Integrates with OpenAI's GPT models to provide intelligent resume content generation and enhancement features.
Containerized with Docker for streamlined deployment.
It serves as an AI-powered component of the Resume Builder application, enabling users to generate and refine resume content through advanced language models, enhancing the overall user experience.
